# Meeting Prep

Generates comprehensive briefing documents for meetings.

## When to Use

- Upcoming client meeting needs prep
- Partner/prospect meeting (PE firms, technology partners, OW)
- Internal planning sessions (L-10, exec planning, 1:1s)
- Conference or event meetings
- Coaching sessions (Kevin Jordan)

## Workflow

### 1. Meeting Context
Identify from calendar or request:
- Meeting type: client / partner / prospect / internal / coaching
- Attendees and their roles
- Agenda (if provided) or likely topics
- History: previous meetings, outcomes, open items

### 2. Attendee Research
For external meetings:
- LinkedIn profiles: role, tenure, background
- Company context: recent news, funding, key initiatives
- Previous interactions: emails, Circleback transcripts, CRM notes
- Connection points: shared contacts, mutual interests

### 3. Context Compilation
Pull from internal sources:
- Recent Circleback transcripts from prior meetings with same attendees
- Open action items from previous meetings
- Relevant Asana tasks or project status
- Any pending proposals, SOWs, or deliverables
- Relevant Slack threads or email exchanges

### 4. Briefing Document

**For client/partner meetings:**
- Attendee profiles (1-2 lines each)
- Relationship status and recent history
- Open items and their current status
- Recommended talking points
- Questions to ask
- Desired outcomes from this meeting
- Watch-outs or sensitive topics

**For internal meetings:**
- Agenda items with context
- Data/metrics needed for each item
- Decisions needed
- Pre-read materials (linked)

**For prospect meetings:**
- Company overview and pain points
- Why Blue Orange is a fit
- Relevant case studies or proof points
- Pricing/engagement model to propose
- Competitive landscape
- Ask: what's the desired next step?

### 5. Pre-Meeting Delivery
- Send briefing doc 2 hours before meeting (or evening before for morning meetings)
- Attach any relevant documents
- Flag if any prep items need Josh's input

## Output Artifacts
- `prep-[meeting-name]-[date].md` — Briefing document
- Linked references and pre-reads

## Trigger
Can be triggered automatically from calendar events or on-demand.
