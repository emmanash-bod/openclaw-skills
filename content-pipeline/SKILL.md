# Content Pipeline

End-to-end content creation and publishing workflow for Blue Orange Digital.

## When to Use

- Creating LinkedIn posts, blog articles, or case studies
- Researching topics for thought leadership content
- Managing the content calendar and publishing cadence
- Repurposing meeting insights or client work into content

## Workflow

### 1. Topic Research
- Scan industry trends: AI/ML, data engineering, cloud platforms
- Review recent client work for case study potential (anonymized)
- Analyze high-performing content from reference sources (e.g., Every newsletter)
- Check content calendar for gaps in coverage
- Generate 3-5 topic suggestions with angle and target audience

### 2. Persona Development
For each piece:
- Define target reader persona (CTO, VP Data, PE partner, etc.)
- Identify their key pain points and questions
- Determine the value proposition of the content
- Select voice and tone (technical depth vs. executive overview)

### 3. Research & Outline
- Deep research on the topic (web search, academic papers, industry reports)
- Create structured outline with key arguments
- Identify data points, statistics, or examples to include
- Note any Blue Orange proof points or case studies to reference

### 4. Draft Creation
- Write first draft following the outline
- Match the voice profile (reference: Every newsletter style for thought leadership)
- Include: hook, problem statement, insight/solution, proof, CTA
- Keep LinkedIn posts to 1200-1500 characters
- Blog posts: 800-1500 words with clear headers

### 5. Review & Refinement
- Self-review against content checklist:
  - [ ] Clear value proposition in first two sentences
  - [ ] No jargon without explanation
  - [ ] Specific examples or data (not generic claims)
  - [ ] Call to action appropriate for the platform
  - [ ] Blue Orange positioning subtle, not salesy
- Route to Dalida for voice/brand review
- Route to Josh for final approval

### 6. Publishing & Distribution
- Format for target platform (LinkedIn, blog, newsletter)
- Schedule or publish
- Cross-post strategy: primary platform first, repurpose for others
- Track engagement metrics

## Content Types

| Type | Length | Cadence | Approver |
|------|--------|---------|----------|
| LinkedIn post | 1200-1500 chars | 2-3x/week | Josh |
| Blog article | 800-1500 words | 1x/week | Josh |
| Case study | 1000-2000 words | 1x/month | Josh + Client |
| Newsletter | 500-800 words | 2x/month | Josh |

## Output Artifacts
- Content draft in target format
- Content Pipeline board task tracking status
- Publishing schedule entry

## Approval Gate
All external content requires Josh review before publish.
